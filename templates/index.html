About Laamasia

Laa Masia Football Club was formed on 22th August 2021. We have been practising Tikitaka style of football that will be an asset to the future generations. Laa Macia FC currently pooseses teams under 12, 13, 15, 18, reserve and senior.

Our aim is to help the nation by molding next world class players. The financially struggling members of the club will be provided with right assistance and scholarships. Join our club and let’s cruise together to the world of football.
