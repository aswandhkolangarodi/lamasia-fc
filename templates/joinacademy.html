Best Soccer Club in Kerala

Laa Masia FC Kerala Football Club was established on 22nd August 2021 at Kuriyodam in Malappuram District of Kerala. The club was initially started purely as a professional soccer training academy. The academy was later transformed into a club by Aman Roshan, who is the head coach and co-founder of Laa Macia FC Kerala Football Club. The motto of the company is to mould skilled soccer players and paving way for them to participate in the best soccer tournaments like KPL (Kerala Premier League), ISL (Indian Super League) and I League. The club also offers assistance and scholarships for financially rearward players.
